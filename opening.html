Prospective Students

We are looking for motivated students interested in autonomous systems, cyber-physical security, machine learning, and aerial drone applications. A background in control theory, robotics, and programming (C/C++, MATLAB/Simulink, Python, ROS) is preferred. If you are interested to join our group, please e-mail Dr. Hamid Jafarnejad Sani at hjafarne [at] stevens [dot] edu and attach your updated resume/CV.
